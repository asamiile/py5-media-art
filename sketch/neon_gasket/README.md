# neon_gasket

A fusion of ancient fractal geometry and futuristic urbanism. The work reimagines the Apollonian Gasket—a recursive packing of tangent circles—as a blueprint for a high-density, neon-lit metropolis.

## Theme
- Fractal Urbanism
- Cyberpunk
- Geometric Order
- Infinite Detail

## Technique
- **Apollonian Gasket**: A recursive algorithm based on Descartes' Circle Theorem to pack circles within circles.
- **Isometric Projection**: Each circle is projected into a 3D hexagonal prism in an isometric view.
- **Curvature-Height Mapping**: The height of each "building" is inversely proportional to its radius, creating a field of needle-like spires around massive hubs.
- **Neon Aesthetic**: High-contrast colors (Electric Blue, Laser Pink, Cyber Lime) against a pitch-black void.

## Visual Impression
An infinite, glowing city of glass and light. Tiny towers cluster around broad hubs, all following a perfect fractal logic. The "vertical needle" architecture creates a sense of extreme density and high-tech complexity, like a digital cathedral or a massive server farm.
