# avalanche_ledger

An arithmetic avalanche leaves a crisp record of every collapse.

## Concept

The work imagines computation as sedimentary bookkeeping: each local overload spills into its neighbors until the whole field becomes a precise residue map.

## Technique

- **Abelian Sandpile Relaxation**: Large grain deposits repeatedly topple into four-neighbor cells until no cell exceeds the stability threshold.
- **Residue Coloring**: Final cell states `0..3` map to a restrained dark-teal, mauve, and ochre palette.
- **Topple Heat Layer**: The logarithm of toppling frequency adds depth where the avalanche worked hardest.
- **Crisp Nearest Upscaling**: The low-resolution computation is enlarged without blur to preserve the cellular ledger quality.

## Visual Impression

A sharp full-canvas computational field with dark voids, teal/mauve avalanche residues, ochre stress deposits, and fine audit-like hatching.
