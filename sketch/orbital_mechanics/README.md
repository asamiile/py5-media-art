# orbital_mechanics

A generative artwork simulating the complex, mathematical beauty of N-body gravitational orbits.

## Concept
The piece visualizes the "cosmic dance" of gravity. It portrays a multi-planet system where hundreds of satellites are captured in intricate, looping orbits, creating a long-exposure map of celestial motion and gravitational influence.

## Technique
- **N-Body Gravity Simulation**: A multi-body gravitational system is simulated using Newton's law of universal gravitation.
- **RK4 Numerical Integration**: High-precision Runge-Kutta 4th order integration ensures stable and accurate orbital paths over thousands of steps.
- **Velocity-Based Line Weight**: Orbital trails vary in thickness based on local velocity, simulating depth and the kinetic energy of the satellites.
- **Enhanced Celestial Bodies**: Planets feature layered atmospheric glows and the primary body includes a faint, elegant ring system.
- **Long-Exposure Accumulation**: Over 1,500 simulation steps are rendered with semi-transparent lines to create a luminous, ethereal map of orbits.
