# solar_wind

A generative artwork simulating the interaction between solar plasma and a planetary magnetosphere.

## Concept
The piece visualizes the invisible magnetic forces that protect planets from the sun's high-energy solar wind. It captures the dynamic, energetic nature of plasma being channeled and deflected by a magnetic dipole field.

## Technique
- **Vector Field Interaction**: Combines a constant horizontal solar wind flow with a 2D magnetic dipole field and turbulent Perlin noise.
- **Particle Streamlines**: 7,000 particles are integrated through the vector field using Euler integration.
- **Additive Blending**: Multiple overlapping transparent paths create a "glowing plasma" effect with high intensity at convergence points.
- **Cosmic Detail**: A background of thousands of dim, scattered points simulates cosmic radiation and distant stars, adding depth to the scene.
