# kinetic_mandala

A meditation on symmetry and rhythm. The work explores the beauty of recursive geometry in motion, creating a complex "digital mandala" that breathes and rotates with hypnotic precision.

## Theme
- Sacred Geometry
- Rhythmic Symmetry
- Light Meditation
- Kinetic Art

## Technique
- **Recursive Symmetry**: 16-fold rotational symmetry applied to nested geometric seeds.
- **Nested Oscillations**: 7 rings of the mandala rotate and scale with unique frequencies and counter-rotations.
- **High-Density Line Rendering**: Thousands of fine lines with alpha blending create rich, glowing gradients and complex moiré patterns.
- **Additive Blending**: Intersecting lines stack to create "energy points" of high intensity.
- **Hypnotic Motion**: Smooth, sinusoidal modulation of all parameters for a breathing, organic feel.

## Visual Impression
A complex, golden clockwork mechanism floating in a dark charcoal void. It looks like a blossoming flower or a celestial diagram made of pure light. The intricate patterns shift constantly, creating a hypnotic visual rhythm as the layers interact and transform.
