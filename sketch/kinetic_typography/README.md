# kinetic_typography

A generative artwork exploring the boundary between language and abstract digital flow.

## Concept
The piece visualizes the ephemeral nature of digital communication. It portrays a swirling vortex of characters that emerge from chaos to form a semi-legible grid, representing the constant flow and reorganization of data in our modern age.

## Technique
- **Dynamic Text Particles**: 3,000 individual characters (including Latin, Greek, and mathematical symbols) are treated as autonomous agents.
- **Vortex Vector Field**: A powerful central vortex combined with multi-frequency noise drives the initial chaotic movement of the characters.
- **Target Attraction**: Over time, characters are increasingly attracted to a structured grid, creating a transition from abstraction to legibility.
- **Chromatic Aberration & Glitch**: Final rendering includes faint red/blue offsets and horizontal row shifts to simulate a distorted digital display.
- **Multi-layered Trails**: Faint, historical character trails build up background texture and a sense of past motion.
