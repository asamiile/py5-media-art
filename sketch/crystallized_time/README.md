# crystallized_time

A generative artwork simulating shattered crystals and the concept of time frozen in geometry.

## Concept
The piece explores the fragility and complexity of time by imagining it as a shattered crystalline structure. It captures a frozen moment of prismatic light, where every shard represents a fragment of memory or a fleeting second.

## Technique
- **Recursive Polygon Subdivision**: A large rectangular field is recursively sliced using randomized clipping lines to create hundreds of unique "shards."
- **Geometric Filtering**: Shards are filtered by area to ensure a clean, balanced composition with varied scales of detail.
- **SCREEN Blend Luminosity**: Using the `SCREEN` blend mode allows overlapping shards to build up intense, glowing light.
- **Rainbow Diffraction**: Specular edges are rendered with subtle, offset spectral color lines to simulate the prismatic dispersion of light within crystal.
- **Prism Flares**: Enhanced cross-shaped flares with central glints add a sense of brilliant light and atmosphere.
