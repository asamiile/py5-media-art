# Sound Waves

A procedural sound visualization featuring frequency-based wave patterns and spectrogram-style displays.

## Theme

Acoustics, sound visualization, frequency patterns, audio waves.

## Technique

Procedural waveform generation with frequency-based layering, amplitude modulation, harmonic overtones, and spectrogram-style visualization.

## Visual Elements

- **Horizontal Waves**: 8 layered sound waves with harmonic overtones
- **Circular Waves**: 12 radiating circular wave patterns from center
- **Spectrogram**: 20-row frequency spectrum visualization
- **Frequency Bars**: 64-bar spectrum display at bottom
- **Color Palette**: Deep charcoal, electric blue, warm magenta, bright cyan, soft purple

## Parameters

- 8 horizontal wave layers
- 12 circular wave rings
- 20 spectrogram rows
- 64 frequency bars
- Harmonic multipliers: [1, 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, 8, 10]

## Color Scheme

- Deep Charcoal: (20, 20, 25) - Background
- Electric Blue: (0, 150, 255) - Low frequency waves
- Warm Magenta: (255, 50, 150) - Mid frequency waves
- Bright Cyan: (0, 255, 255) - High frequency waves
- Soft Purple: (150, 100, 200) - Accent
- Golden Orange: (255, 165, 0) - Highlight

## Notes

Each render produces a unique sound visualization with randomized wave parameters, harmonic combinations, and frequency distributions. The layered wave patterns create complex interference patterns reminiscent of audio analysis software and oscilloscope displays.
